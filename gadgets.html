Windows Tablet

A major problem for my notebook is about weight. I hate bringing a heavy notebook everyday to school. To solve is problem, I will bring a mini-computer (Sorry, it is not an iPad.) to school to finish my lessons. A Windows Tablet can do things that a normal notebook can do, except I cannot play computer games on it.

This gadget is a handy and trendy thing nowadays. Because of the release of Windows 10, this kind of tablet is being more useful in daily life. It's time to say goodbye to Windows 8.1 on tablet and welcome Windows 10.

Smartphones

The ground-breaking release of the first iPhone has changed the world habit on using mobile phone. Everyone can easily get an Android smartphone with a low price. You can choose from inexpensive and useful Xiaomi to trendy and costly Apple iPhone. For me, I won't follow suit to change my phone very often. Usually I bring two phones to school and go window-shopping. One is my main phone and the other is for tethering. Both phones are pretty powerful and their performance are not outdated (for me).
